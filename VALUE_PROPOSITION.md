# Value Proposition Canvas: CreatorNexus AI

## Customer Profile: The Modern Stock Creator
*   **Jobs to be Done:** Tagging photos/videos, writing SEO descriptions, researching trending keywords, uploading to multiple agencies, tracking sales performance.
*   **Pains:** Hours spent on repetitive data entry, "Keywording Fatigue," low visibility due to poor SEO, difficulty tracking what sells where.
*   **Gains:** Passive income growth, more time to create new content, global reach, professional-level metadata.

## Our Solution (Value Map)
*   **Products & Services:**
    *   AI Auto-Tagging & Captioning (Computer Vision).
    *   Smart Multi-Platform Uploader.
    *   Predictive Trend Dashboard.
*   **Pain Relievers:**
    *   Eliminate manual typing with "One-Click Metadata."
    *   Bulk processing engine for thousands of assets.
    *   Automated rejection analysis (AI suggests fixes for common stock agency rejections).
*   **Gain Creators:**
    *   "Profit-Lens" feature: Suggests what to shoot next based on real-time market gaps.
    *   Dynamic Keyword Optimization: Adjusts tags based on seasonal trends automatically.
